
Gases
a. discharge through gases (qualitative treatment only)
b. application of conduction of electricity through gases

Candidates should be able to:
i. distinguish between electrolytes and nonelectrolytes;
ii. analyse the processes of electrolysis;
iii. apply Faraday’s laws of electrolysis to
solve problems;
iv. analyse discharge through gases;
v. determine some applications/uses of
conduction of electricity through gases.
