Introduction to Fibre Optics & Lasers
a. fibre optics
b. Lasers


Candidates should be able to;
(i) explain the concept of fibre optics;
(ii) understand the principle of
transmission of light through an
optical fibre;
(iii) apply the principle of fibre optics in
Local Area Network (LAN), medicine,
laser beam etc;

Candidates should be able to;
(i) understand the meaning of laser;
(ii) understand the various types of lasers
(solid state, gas, liquid and semiconductor
lasers);
(iii) apply the knowledge of lasers in
scientific research, communication,
medicine, military technology, holograms
etc;
(iv) identify the dangers involved in
using lasers.
