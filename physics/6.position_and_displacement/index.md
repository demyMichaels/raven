b) Measurement, position, distance and displacement

(a) concept of displacement;
(ii) distinction between distance and displacement;
(iii) concept of position and coordinates;
(iv) frame of reference.



Candidates should be able to:
i. use strings, meter rule and
engineering calipers, vernier
calipers and micrometer, screw
guage;
ii. note the degree of accuracy;
iii. identify distance travel in a
specified direction;
iv. use compass and protractor to
locate points/directions;
v. use Cartesians systems to locate
positions in x-y plane;
vi. plot graph and draw inference
from the graph.
