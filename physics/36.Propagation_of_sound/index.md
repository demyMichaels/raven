
Propagation of sound
i. the necessity for a material medium 
ii. Speech of sound in solids, liquids and air,
iii. Reflection of sound; echoes, reverbation and their applications
iv. Advantages and disadvantages of echoes and reverbations

Candidates should be able to:
i. determine the need for a material
medium in the propagation of sound
waves;
ii. compare the speed of sound in solids,
liquids and air;
iii. relate the effects of temperature and pressure to the speed of sound in air;
iv. solve problem on echoes, reverberation
and speed;
v. compare the disadvantages and
advantages of echoes.
