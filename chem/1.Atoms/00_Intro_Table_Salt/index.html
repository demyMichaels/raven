1.0 The Mystery of Table Salt

An Introduction to Chemical Reactions

Sodium is a soft, silvery metal that reacts violently with moisture. Chlorine is a poisonous, greenish-yellow gas.

🔥 When molten sodium meets chlorine gas, it bursts into flame, producing a white powder – common table salt!

Two toxic substances combine to form something we eat every day. How does this happen?

🤔 How do we explain the differences in properties of different forms of matter? How do chemical reactions like this work?
