figures.

formula?

b. If you ran the reaction and it produced 5.0 mol H2O, how
many moles of both O2 and H2 did you start with?
c. If you started with 37.5 g O2, how many grams of H2 did
you start with to have a complete reaction?
d. How many grams of O2 and H2 were reacted to produce
30.0 g H2O?

a. How many moles of Fe2O3 are required to completely react

3.14 mol Fe2O3 and 99.1 g Al?
c. How many atoms of Al are required to produce 7.0 g Fe?

reaction is completed.
c. Calculate the theoretical yield of the reaction. How many
grams of material would you expect to obtain if the reaction
has a 47.2% yield?
