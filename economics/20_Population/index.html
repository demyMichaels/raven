Population a. Meaning and theories; b. Census: importance and problems. c. Size and growth: over-population, under- population and optimum population. d. Structure and distribution; e. Population policy and economic development Candidates should be able to: (i) analyse some population theories: (ii) examine the relevance of the theories to Nigeria; (iii) examine the uses and limitations of census data; (iv) identify determinants of the size, composition and growth of population; (v) analyse the structure and distribution of population; (vi) appraise government population policy in Nigeria.
