This simulation puts you in the role of a CEO for a Japanese electronics company from 2000 to 2020. You'll make strategic decisions about R&D investment, manufacturing, market expansion, and product lines to see if you can navigate the challenges faced by Japanese electronics firms during this period.

Key features:

Interactive dashboard showing your company's metrics over time
Comparison with competitors like Samsung and Apple
News feed with industry developments that influence the market
Strategic decisions that impact your company's performance
Detailed feedback on your decisions and their consequences
The simulation emphasizes the core dilemma mentioned in your scenario: whether to continue focusing on traditional, commoditized products or pivot to innovation and exit unprofitable segments. Your decisions will clearly show how these strategies impact profit, market share, and innovation over time.
