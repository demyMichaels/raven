Setup Steps

1

Zero the balance

Move all riders to zero, adjust leveling screw until pointer is at zero

2

Place object

Gently place the object on the left pan

3

Adjust riders

Start with largest beam, work to smallest until balanced

Reading Guide

Hundreds beam: Read the notch position (0, 100, 200, 300, 400, 500g)

Tens beam: Read the notch position (0-90g in 10g increments)

Grams beam: Read the scale position (0-10g in 0.1g increments)

Total mass = Sum of all three readings

Module 3: Physics of Beam Balance (Torque & Equilibrium)

Understand the physics principles that make beam balances work.

Watch: Torque and a Balance Beam

Learn how torque and equilibrium principles apply to beam balances.

Torque (τ)

Torque = Force × Distance from pivot

The rotational force that causes objects to turn around a pivot point.

Formula: τ = F × d

Equilibrium

When clockwise torque = counterclockwise torque

The balance is stable when opposing torques are equal.

Condition: Στ = 0
