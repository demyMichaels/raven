12. THE ELECTORAL SYSTEM

12.1 PERFORMANCE OBJECTIVES
At the end of this chapter, students should be able to:
(1) Explain the meaning of elections.
(2) Explain the meaning of suffrage.
(3) Trace the development of suffrage.
(4) Identify the types of suffrage.


(5) Discuss the meaning of franchise.
(6) Enumerate the purpose served by elections in various political systems.

(7) List the types of electoral systems.
(8) Identify the characteristics of each system.
(9) Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of each system.
(10) Explain the meaning of free and fair elections.
(11) Mention the stages in conducting elections.


(12) Identify electoral commission.
(13) List the functions of an electoral commission.
(14) Enumerate the roles of the various electoral officials.
